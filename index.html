About us


Having realized the potential for quality and reliable IT products and services in the regions of East Africa and South Asia, DataCore was formed with a group of global citizens and several leading technologists in Africa, Europe and Asia. The object of the formalization of this partnership was simply to deliver quality and best in class; IT products & services at an affordable price with guaranteed 24×7 support.


DataCore was setup in 2010 as an aviation IT company, focused on building, mission critical applications in Nairobi, Kenya (East African Region). By now the company has come a long way and provides diversified IT solutions for all industries & business areas. DataCore Technologies has grown in to an end to end digital tech company specialized in cutting edge technologies.


Sri Lanka is the heart of the business and functions as the dedicated development and resource centre in collaboration with consultants and partners from Canada, Switzerland, United Kingdom, China, Hong Kong, Malaysia, Singapore, Thailand, Pacific Islands and the East Africa Region.

Software Devlopment

We deliver comprehensive support for both open source and commercial technologies. Mission Critical Software Applications are the priority for any business in today’s internet economy. Our value proposition is to simplify and streamline business processes and reduce cost. This we believe is a key driver to any successful business. To be successful you require clear visibility across enterprise business processes, as well as the technical and cost dimensions of software application systems. We provide a large development center focusing on Cloud Applications, Client-Server and Mobile. Our core development team includes the industry's finest, our pricing methodology follow SaaS (Software as a Service)/ Transactional Processing and we are now looking to explore new technologies including IoT and Block-Chain. DataCore’s proprietary Advanced Application Development Framework helps understand and deliver solutions to you by converging the right set of applications, middleware and databases with the best delivery channels. System Intergration

Similar to our software framework our approach to integration of our customers systems is based on the right kind of technology for the business requirement thereby creating responsive and scalable IT infrastructure on demand to support the critical needs of our customers with guaranteed 24x7 support. Our technology expertise spans across application architecture, hardware refresh, middleware, data center services, storage solutions, security, managed services and multiple levels of “white label” support services for all major brands in the market. Leveraging proven methodologies and tools along with professionals with quality industry expertise our service offering ranges from standard pc helpdesk services and program management to complex implementations for enterprise level networks and datacenters. From concept to design to implementation DataCore will add value to all our customers. Our certifications, collaboration and partnerships with all global brands will help create an integrated, company-wide technology platform which will enable our customers to build practical and cost efficient business and technology frameworks which will drive them to making their missions and visions a success.
